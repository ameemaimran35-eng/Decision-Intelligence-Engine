How to Use

Decision Intelligence Engine
1

Type your decision

In the simulator search box, type any real life decision you're facing — career, relationships, finances, education, or anything else. Be specific for better results.

💡 Example: "Should I quit my job to start a freelance business?"
2

Press Enter or click Simulate

Hit Enter or click the Simulate Future button. The AI will analyze thousands of possible outcomes and return results in seconds.

3

Read your results

You'll see three main paths: Best Path Most Likely Risk Path — each with probability scores, timelines, and emotional forecasts.

4

Explore extended analysis

Scroll down past the main results to see AI Analysis, Future Timeline, Emotional Forecast, and Recommended Missions — all generated specifically for your decision.

5

Auto-reset when done

Click anywhere outside the simulator or navigate to another section — the simulator automatically resets and is ready for your next decision.

1

Enter Compare Mode

Click the ⚔ Compare button beside the Simulate button. The simulator switches to a two-input layout.

2

Enter two decisions

Type Decision A in the top input and Decision B in the bottom input. Both decisions are simulated independently at the same time.

💡 Example A: "Start a business" · Example B: "Get a corporate job"
3

Run the comparison

Click ⚔ Run Comparison. Both decisions are sent to the AI simultaneously and results appear side by side.

4

Read the battle result

A winner is declared based on 5 metrics: Success Probability, Opportunity Score, Readiness, Risk Level, and Confidence. The option with more wins is recommended.

5

Exit compare mode

Click ✕ Exit Compare to return to normal simulation mode at any time.

1

Adjust your habit sliders

Scroll to the Life Tracker section. Move each slider to reflect your real daily habits — Sleep, Study, Screen Time, Exercise, Productivity, Social, and Mental Wellness.

2

Watch scores update live

Your Health, Focus, Productivity, Happiness, Discipline, and Mental scores update in real time as you move the sliders. The overall score and XP bar also update instantly.

3

Save your daily snapshot

Click 💾 Save to record today's scores. This creates a snapshot in your Life Journey history so you can track improvement over time.

4

View your journey

Click 🌌 Journey to open a slide panel showing all your saved snapshots with score bars, dates, and filter options by week or month.

5

Read your analytics

The Analytics section shows your score trends as a line chart, your personal bests, averages per category, and current streak count.

1

What the Galaxy Map represents

The Galaxy Map is a visual representation of your life habits. Each glowing star represents a different life area — Sleep, Study, Exercise, Productivity, Social, Screen Time, and Mental Wellness.

2

Click any star to inspect it

Clicking a star opens a detail panel showing your current score for that habit, what it means, and how improving it affects your overall life score.

3

The central orb

The large glowing orb in the center represents your overall life score. Its color changes based on your Cosmic Mood — purple for balanced, green for thriving, red for burnout.

4

Locked vs unlocked stars

Some stars are locked until you improve specific habits. Raise your Life Tracker scores to unlock more stars and expand your galaxy.

5

Personality Evolution

Below the galaxy you'll see your Personality Type — updated live based on your habit combination. Adjust your sliders to evolve toward different personality archetypes.

1

Automatic saving

Every simulation you complete is automatically saved to your Decision History. No manual action needed — it happens in the background after each simulation.

2

Open your history

Click the 📜 History button in the simulator area. A slide panel opens from the right showing all your past simulations.

3

Search and sort

Use the search box to find a specific decision by keyword. Sort by Newest, Oldest, Highest Score, or Lowest Score using the dropdown.

4

Replay a past decision

Click ↩ Replay on any history card to load that decision back into the simulator input. Then click Simulate to re-run it with your current settings.

5

Delete or clear history

Delete individual entries with the 🗑 button, or clear all history at once. All history is stored locally in your browser — it never leaves your device.
